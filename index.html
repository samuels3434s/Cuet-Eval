How to Save Response Sheet

  1. Open your Response Sheet on your computer.
  2. Click the three dots in the top-right corner of your browser.
  3. Click on Cast, save and share.
  4. Click on Save page as... (or press Ctrl + S).
  5. In the save dialog, click the format dropdown and select "Webpage, HTML Only".
  6. Click Save.
  7. Upload that saved HTML file using the button below.

How to Copy Answer Key

  1. Log in to your portal and open the Answer Key challenge page.
  2. Select and highlight the contents of the answer key table (or press Ctrl + A to select everything on the page).
  3. Copy it to your clipboard by pressing Ctrl + C.
  4. Paste the copied text directly into the textbox on this page (Ctrl + V).
  5. Note: The evaluator will automatically scan the pasted text to find and pair up the Question IDs and Correct Option IDs.
